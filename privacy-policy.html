Privacy Policy for Silicon Tycoon

Last Updated: November 27, 2024

Overview

Silicon Tycoon ("the Game") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage.

Data Collection

Silicon Tycoon does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

What We Don't Collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Advertising data

Local Data Storage

The Game saves your progress locally on your device using:

  • Local Storage: Game saves, settings, and achievements
  • All data stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers

Third-Party Services

Silicon Tycoon does NOT use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Cloud storage services
  • Any third-party tracking

Children's Privacy

Our Game does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. The Game is suitable for all ages and does not require any personal information to play.

Data Security

Since we don't collect or transmit data, all your game progress is stored securely on your device. You can delete all game data by:

  • Uninstalling the app
  • Clearing app data in your device settings

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • All game data is stored locally on your device
  • You can delete your data at any time
  • No data is shared with third parties

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date above
  • Posting a notice in the Game (if applicable)

Continued use of the Game after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
